    Citizen hodari is a civicTech project tracker that aims to collect factual information about city Projects from initialization to completion and form database usable as a point of reference to various interested entities especially the general public.Currently, the information on the county websites about projects cannot suffice to hold people who oversee project implementations accountable in the event doubts and questions arise. With accurate,easily accesible data,following up on city projects to both manage and maintain public assets and inform policies around developments.
